The Team Behind the Shield

Upper Shield Roofing is a family-operated roofing company built on integrity, experience, and strong relationships. Our founder brings over 10 years of experience in the construction industry, with the past 4 years dedicated specifically to storm restoration across Texas, Colorado, and Wyoming.
