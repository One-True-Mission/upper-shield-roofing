What We Offer

Roofing & Storm Restoration Services

Every service below starts the same way: a free inspection and an honest answer about what your property actually needs.

Free 35-Point Inspection

Our zero-cost, 35-point property damage inspection documents hail and wind damage in detail. Much of the damage that matters to your claim isn't visible from the ground, and our job is to find it, photograph it, and put it on record.

Insurance Claim Assistance

Insurance claims are where good roofs go to get denied. We guide you through filing, meet your adjuster on-site to walk the damage together, and provide completion documentation so your claim closes clean.

Storm Damage Restoration

Once your claim is approved, we complete repairs or a full replacement per your insurance scope. Built to city code, documented through completion, and finished by the same team that inspected it.

Roof Replacement

When repair isn't enough, we replace the whole system with quality materials and careful installation. Your new roof meets city code and is built to protect what matters most for years to come.

Roof Repair

Missing shingles, slow leaks, flashing failures. We track the problem to its source and fix it properly, so a small repair today doesn't become a full replacement next year.

Gutters, Siding & Windows

Hail doesn't stop at the shingles. We handle gutters, siding, and windows as part of your restoration, so the entire exterior gets finished by one contractor on one timeline.

Claim Denied?

A denial is not always the final answer.

Adjusters miss damage. Claims get filed with incomplete details. We've seen denied claims turn into fully approved roof replacements after a second inspection uncovered what the first one missed.

If your claim was denied or you're unsure about storm damage, don't give up. Request a free second inspection and let us take a look before you accept the denial.
